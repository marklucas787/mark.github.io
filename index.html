My Story

My journey began in the early days of business software development, where I discovered my passion for creating solutions that make people's work lives easier. Over three decades, I've evolved alongside technology – from early database systems to today's modern Power Platform, always guided by the belief that technology should serve people, not the other way around. What drives me is seeing the impact of my work: the relief in a client's eyes when a manual process that took days now takes minutes, or the excitement when teams can finally access insights they've needed for years. My mission is to empower organizations with custom applications that don't just solve problems but transform how work gets done. I value transparency, craftsmanship, and lasting relationships. I believe in building solutions that stand the test of time and in being a trusted advisor, not just a developer. Every project is an opportunity to apply my expertise in ways that create genuine, meaningful improvements for the people who will use what I build.

Throughout my career, I've helped organizations across diverse sectors – from government agencies like NASA and DOJ to private enterprises – leverage technology to overcome their most pressing challenges. My expertise spans the Microsoft ecosystem, with particular focus on SharePoint, Power Apps, and SQL Server integration. What sets my work apart is a blend of technical mastery and business sense. I don't just code solutions; I architect them with an eye toward scalability, security, and user adoption. My approach combines the precision of a developer with the strategic thinking of a consultant, ensuring that what I build aligns perfectly with both immediate needs and long-term goals. I'm Microsoft certified in Power Platform Fundamentals and hold multiple SharePoint certifications, but my greatest qualification is the practical wisdom gained from hundreds of successful implementations. I've seen firsthand what works, what doesn't, and how to navigate the complexities of enterprise technology environments to deliver results that exceed expectations.
